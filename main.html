John Felix Anthony Cena Jr.

A Wrestling Superstar

Timeline

  • 1977 - Born in West Newbury, Massachusetts
  • 1999 - Begins training to become a professional wrestler at Ultimate Pro Wrestling in California
  • 2001 - Signs a developmental contract with the World Wrestling Federation, assigned to Ohio Valley Wrestling
  • 2002 - Makes his WWE television debut on Smackdown! against Kurt Angle. Instantly became a fan favorite
  • 2004 - Wins his first singles championship in the WWE, the United States Championship
  • 2005 - Wins his first world championship, the WWE Championship
  • 2013 - For the next 2 years, John Cena would embark on multiple championship reigns which would sour opinions on him as the face of the company
  • 2015 - Takes a step back from the limelight to elevate the status of the United States Championship
  • 2019 - Begins taking a step back from professional wrestling to work on other endeavors such as his acting career
